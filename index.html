Our Impact

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to assisting refugees who have been resettled in Arizona. Our goal is to create a welcoming environment and provide the tools necessary for success and integration. We believe in the strength and resilience of every individual, and work to empower all for a brighter future. This is who we are-we are of one heart.
